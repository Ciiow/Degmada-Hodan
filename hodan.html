Taariikhda Degmada Hodan

Degmada hodan waxaa degmo ahaan loo aqoonsaday sanadkii 1973 waxay Qeeb ahaan kutaalaa afarta qeybood ee gobalka banaadir qeebtiisa wilyoow cade
